# Backtest Research Summary

This project was prepared from a private research workflow focused on XAUUSD/gold Strategy Tester experiments.

The historical research showed promising behavior in selected multi-year backtests, but this public repository does not include raw reports, real presets, optimization files or broker exports.

## What Is Included

- A source-only Expert Advisor for independent review.
- High-level methodology notes.
- Risk and validation limitations.
- Sanitized example inputs.

## What Is Not Included

- Raw MT5 HTML/XML reports.
- Real `.set` presets.
- Broker data.
- Account data.
- Optimization grids.
- Production parameters.
- Private research logs.

## Interpretation Limits

Backtest results depend on:

- data quality;
- spread;
- slippage;
- commission;
- broker feed;
- execution model;
- market regime;
- overfitting risk;
- parameter stability;
- out-of-sample validation;
- walk-forward validation;
- Monte Carlo testing;
- real execution costs.

Backtests can be useful for research transparency, but they do not guarantee future performance.
